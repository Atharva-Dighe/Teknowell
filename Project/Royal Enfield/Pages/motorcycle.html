Hunter 350

The RE Hunter 350, a roadster-style motorcycle known for its neo-retro design, accessible seat height, and urban-friendly features.Designed easy to ride, with comfortable riding posture, nimble handling, making it well-suited for city commutes and everyday use.

Scram 440

The RE Scram 440, a scrambler-styled motorcycle launched in January 2025, & is based on the popular Himalayan platform. It features a larger, 443cc, air/oil-cooled engine producing 25.4bhp and 34Nm of torque, paired with a 6-speed gearbox.

Himalayan 450

The Royal Enfield Himalayan 450 is an adventure touring motorcycle, built to handle both on-road and off-road terrains, featuring a new 452cc liquid-cooled engine producing 40bhp and 42Nm of torque, paired with a 6-speed gearbox.

Continental GT 650

The Royal Enfield Continental GT 650 is a retro-styled cafe racer motorcycle built on the company's 650cc parallel-twin platform. It features a sporty riding position, clip-on handlebars, and rear-set footrests.
